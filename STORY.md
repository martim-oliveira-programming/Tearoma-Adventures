## Path 2: Stay with Mom

**[NAME]:** You're right Mom. I'm sorry. Let's wait for the police together. I love you.

You stay with your Mom and try to comfort her as best as you can. The police arrive a few hours later and start investigating your Dad's disappearance. You tell them everything you know and they promise to do their best to find him.

The next day, the police inform you that they will search the mine your Dad was working at since it's the last place he was seen. You insist on going and your Mom decides to come with you to keep you safe. Because the police need someone that knows your Dad to identify him if they find him, they let you both go.

At the mine entrance, you see the security guards and explain the situation. They let you in and one of them comes with you. As you advance in the tunnels, you start to see a **purple light** that illuminates the cave.

You keep going and the light gets stronger and stronger until you reach a big cavern where you see a group of people that have removed a big rock off what seems to be the light's source.

**[NAME]:** Excuse me! What are you doing?

**Miner:** Some of our crew entered what looked like some kind of secret passage when this giant rock fell and trapped them inside. We just got the rock out and there's this purple passage. No one has had the courage to go through it!!

**[NAME]:** Do you know if one of them is called Luca Lanister?

**Miner:** Unfortunately yes. Mr. Lanister is one of the trapped people. He was the one overseeing the operation.

The police and miners look at each other and then at you.

**Police Officer:** Look kid, we are really sorry about your dad but you can't get near that thing. No one knows what it is!!

**[NAME]:** But my Dad is in there!! Please I have to go in!

You insist and beg them to let you go in but they refuse. You feel helpless and frustrated. Suddenly, your Mom, out of nowhere, steps forward and dives in, going through the purple portal.

---

### ❔ Choice:
> I have to go after her right?!!
> *(Go / Stay)*

### If you go:

**Mom:** I'm going in! I have to find him!

**[NAME]:** Mom no! I'm going in!

The police try to stop both of you but were too late. Both you and your Mom enter the portal. You feel a strange sensation in your body as you pass through it.

*(+2 Speed)*

### If you stay:

You decide not to go after your Mom and hope that she will be ok. You wait for her to come back but she doesn't. After a while, you start to worry and try to find a way to go after her but you don't know how and the police won't let you go back there.

You feel helpless and scared but you have to stay strong and hope that your Mom will be ok and that the police will find her soon. After a while the police organize a search party. As they're going in you follow unnoticed.

*(+1 Stealth)*

*(+13 Hunger)*
